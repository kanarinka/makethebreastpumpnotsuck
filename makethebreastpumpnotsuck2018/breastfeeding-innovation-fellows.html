Breastfeeding Innovation Fellows

Thanks to the generosity of our funders and sponsors we are able to run the Breastfeeding Innovation Fellowship program which brings a national group of talented, brilliant people to our event. Breastfeeding Innovators have experience in lactation support, design, community leadership, advocacy, film & photography and more. Some are accomplished national experts and some are young people with tremendous potential.
